Lasagna Recipe

Description

Prep Time:30mins Cook Time:2 hrs 30 mins Additional Time:15 mins Total Time:3hrs Servings:12.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Cheeses
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
